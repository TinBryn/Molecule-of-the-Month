Welcome to the Molecule of the Month Web AR application

How it works:

1. Click the 'Open app' button above

2. Focus your camera on the Hiro marker, then the molecule of the month will be rendered on top of it

3. You can also rotate the molecule, zoom in and zoom out

Please note: As this is a pure marker-based AR application, the marker should always be visible to the camera
